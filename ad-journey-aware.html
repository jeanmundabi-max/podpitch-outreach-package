Key Principles
What makes this journey convert
Congruence
The ad promise matches the page headline matches the CTA
Visitors confirm "I'm in the right place" within 3 seconds of landing. No disconnect between what was promised and what is shown.
Segmentation
Every word says "agency" not "everyone"
The ad targets agencies. The page speaks to agencies. The testimonials are from agencies. The comparison is against agency alternatives. No mixed messaging.
Temperature
Cold to Warm to Hot. Each step earns the next.
The ad earns the click. The page earns the demo. The thank-you page earns the show-up. No step asks for more commitment than the previous step earned.
