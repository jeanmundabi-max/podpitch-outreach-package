What agencies ask before signing up
Will the pitches actually sound like my client, or like ChatGPT?+
You provide writing samples during guest profile setup. PodPitch analyses tone, vocabulary, sentence structure, and messaging style. Each pitch is generated uniquely per client. Hosts consistently say the emails feel personal. "We don't look like robots anymore." — Sarah Spiegel, Sarah PR.
How is this different from PodMatch or other matching platforms?+
PodMatch is a closed ecosystem. You can only match with hosts who are PodMatch members. PodPitch has 3.85 million podcasts. You reach any podcast with a public contact, not just the ones on a matching network. And you send from your client's real email, not a platform inbox.
We currently use a podcast booking agency. How does PodPitch compare?+
Booking agencies like Kitcaster charge $2,100/mo for approximately 3 bookings per client. PodPitch users average 7 to 9 bookings per month at a fraction of the cost. You keep control of the messaging, the relationship, and the data. And it sends from your client's email, not the agency's.
How many clients can I manage on one account?+
Unlimited. Each client gets their own guest profile with separate voice settings, podcast targets, and reporting. Your team manages everything from one dashboard. Unlimited team members at no extra cost.
What if a client is in a niche industry?+
The database has 3.85 million podcasts across every category. PodPitch uses AI matching to find shows that actually cover your client's topic, audience, and geographic market. Even niche industries typically have 200+ relevant podcasts.
Can I generate reports for client meetings?+
Yes. Export unbranded Excel reports showing pitches sent, open rates, reply rates, and bookings secured. White-label ready for your client meetings. Show them the earned media your agency is delivering.
Is there a minimum contract or commitment?+
No long-term contracts. Month-to-month. Scale up when you add clients, scale down if you need to. No penalties. No lock-in. Start with one client profile and expand when you see the bookings come in.
