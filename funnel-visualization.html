Broadcast · Ongoing · Sent to all subscribers 5x per week
From: Parker Olson <parker@podpitch.com>
How [Name] got booked on 3 shows in 10 days
Broadcast
Parker writes 5 unique broadcast emails per week. Each goes to everyone on the list — no-shows, post-call unconverted, trial users, past customers. Format rotates: → Case study (real user result, specific numbers) → Insight (one thing most people get wrong about podcast pitching) → Social proof (screenshot, quote, booking confirmation) → VSL rebook CTA (for no-shows still in the funnel) → Content (YouTube clip, article, tip) Purpose: keep PodPitch top of mind until the prospect is ready to buy. Most SaaS purchases happen on the 5th to 12th touchpoint.
✓The broadcast layer means no prospect is ever truly lost. They stay on the list receiving fresh content until they buy, unsubscribe, or start a trial. This is the compounding asset that makes the whole funnel work at scale.
