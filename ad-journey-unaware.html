Two Journeys Compared
Same product. Different buyer mindset. Different path.
Aware Journey (3 Steps)
Ad goes to product landing page. Demo CTA immediately.
Buyer already knows they need a podcast PR tool. They're comparing options. Send them to the agency page with comparison table and demo booking. Close fast.
Unaware Journey (4 Steps)
Ad goes to free scorecard. Nurture educates. Demo at Day 10.
Buyer knows they're drowning in manual work but doesn't know a tool exists. Lead with the problem. Educate with the scorecard and playbook. Earn the demo through value. Close rate is higher because the prospect is pre-educated.
